Our Binary MLM Plan

Structure of Binary MLM Plan

Reseller A buys a package from the Company. He recruited 2 resellers to be positioned to his left and right (B&C). He earned Php1,000 Direct Sales Commission from the 2. His recruits (B&C) can earn the same DSC, if B recruits 2 (D&E) and C recruits 2 (F&G). If D & E and F & G recruited 2 each, they will each earn Php1,000.00 DSC. The 4th level will be full and A will be flushed-out to be promoted and will earn a group sales commission of Php20,000.00 plus other privileges.
